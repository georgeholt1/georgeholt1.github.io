Overview

I am currently a Senior Artificial Intelligence Researcher at the STFC Hartree Centre (STFC, UK). My work involves full-stack applied AI research:

  • Machine learning research applied to the physical sciences (nuclear fusion for energy generation, materials discovery for carbon capture and sequestration, small molecule discovery for drug application, etc.);
  • Artificial intelligence research (deep learning, hyperparameter optimisation, active learning, reinforcement learning, etc.);
  • Training machine learning models at scale on high-performance computing and cloud infrastructure;
  • Explaining and interpreting machine learning model behaviour and predictions;
  • Data analysis, engineering and visualisation;
  • Developing and using numerical simulation models of physical systems (tokamak fusion reactor scrape-off layer and divertor, tokamak plasma shape).

I regularly publish my work in internationally leading conferences and journals in AI and physical sciences venues and have received several awards for research excellence.

I received a PhD in Physics from the University of Strathclyde in 2022, a masters in Fusion Energy with distinction and project prize from the University of York in 2018, and an undergraduate degree in Physics from the University of Glasgow in 2017.

I use this site to post about projects I'm involved in, topics I find interesting and solutions to problems I've found that others might find useful. My posts are collected by year here.

Please feel free to reach out via any of the contact methods listed on the contact page.
